PFD Experience Blog

This blog will showcase my entire experience for the entire Portfolio Development (PFD) Module that was done in Year 2 Semester 2 of my diploma study. PFD module allows us to tackle a challenge statement and to make use of full stack development to create a solution for the challenge statement. To tackle the challenge statement I had entered - How can we create greater awareness for sustainability to students and lecturers in Ngee Ann Polytechnic through the use of technology and help them to adopt a more sustainable lifestyle, my group and I decided to make a "Carousell-like" website for the people in Ngee Ann Polytechnic.
